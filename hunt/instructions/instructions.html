Welcome to the Treasure Hunt!

Game Instructions

Objective

Your goal is to solve clues and complete challenges to find the hidden treasure and have fun whilst shopping too. Follow the clues carefully and enjoy the adventure!

Instructions

  • Each clue/challenge is provided with a free hint and a paid hint.
  • After submitting the answer, make sure you upload a proper photo.
  • Rewards will be given depending on your progress.
  • Do not tamper with or alter any clues or game materials.
  • Respect the local rules and guidelines of the location where the treasure hunt takes place.
  • In case of emergencies, contact the event organizers immediately.

Dos and Don'ts

Do:

  • Read each clue carefully.
  • Try solving the clues without using Google for help.
  • Stay within the designated areas.
  • Have fun and be adventurous!

Don't:

  • Do not engage in disruptive behavior.
  • Do not cheat or use unauthorized tools.
  • Do not interfere with other participants.
  • Do not post any of the contents on any other platform or source.

Further actions will be taken against those who misuse or misbehave during the event.
